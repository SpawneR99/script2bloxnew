Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

⚠ YOUR PRIVACY IS OUR TOP PRIORITY

Script2Blox will NEVER ask for your Roblox password, email, personal information, or any account credentials. We only ask for your Roblox username — which is public information visible to everyone. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. If someone asks for your password claiming to be Script2Blox — it is a scam.

What We Collect

  • Roblox Username (public info)
  • Anonymous usage analytics

What We NEVER Collect

  • Passwords
  • Email Addresses
  • Real Names
  • Phone Numbers
  • Financial Data
  • Device Data / IP Logs

1. Information We Collect

Script2Blox operates on a strict data minimization principle. We collect the absolute minimum amount of information required to provide our services:

  • Roblox Username — When you use our key generation system, we ask for your Roblox username. This is publicly available information that anyone can see on the Roblox platform. We use it solely for the purpose of generating your activation key.
  • Anonymous Analytics — We use privacy-respecting analytics (Umami) to understand how people use our website. This data is fully anonymized and contains no personally identifiable information. We track page views and general usage patterns to improve our service.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be absolutely clear, Script2Blox does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Roblox Passwords — We will never ask for your Roblox password. Our services do not require it.
  • Email Addresses — We do not request or store email addresses.
  • Personal Identity — No real names, dates of birth, addresses, or government IDs.
  • Phone Numbers — We do not request or store phone numbers.
  • Financial Information — No credit cards, bank accounts, or payment details. Script2Blox is 100% free.
  • Roblox Account Data — We cannot and do not access your Roblox inventory, Robux balance, friends list, or any other account data.
  • Cookies for Tracking — We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels to follow you across the internet.

3. How We Use Your Username

Your Roblox username is used exclusively for:

  • Generating your Delta Executor activation key
  • Displaying your avatar during the setup process (fetched from Roblox's public API)

Your username is not stored in any database after your session ends. It is processed in real-time and discarded.

4. Third-Party Services

Our verification process may redirect you to third-party offer walls provided by our advertising partner (AdBlueMedia). These third parties have their own privacy policies, and Script2Blox is not responsible for data you provide to them. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before completing any offers.

We use Umami Analytics, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics platform that does not collect personal data or track individual users.

5. Data Security

We protect data through:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all connections
  • No persistent storage of user data
  • Server-side API proxying to prevent exposure of sensitive endpoints
  • Regular security audits

6. Children's Privacy

Script2Blox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we only collect publicly available Roblox usernames and no personal data, our service is designed with privacy for all ages in mind.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is typically nothing to delete or export. However, if you have any privacy concerns, please contact us and we will address them promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our community channels or the platform.
